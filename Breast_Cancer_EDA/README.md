# 🎀 Global Breast Cancer Statistics & Awareness Dataset (2022-2025)

> **Every Story is Unique, Every Journey Matters** — WHO Breast Cancer Awareness Month 2025

## 📌 Overview

Breast cancer is the **most commonly diagnosed cancer** among women worldwide. This dataset provides comprehensive statistics on breast cancer incidence, mortality, survival, risk factors, and treatment access across **50 countries**, supporting the WHO Global Breast Cancer Initiative (GBCI) and its **60-60-80 targets**.

## 📊 Dataset Contents

| File | Records | Description |
|------|---------|-------------|
| `breast_cancer_by_country.csv` | 50 countries | Incidence, mortality, survival, screening coverage, treatment access |
| `breast_cancer_risk_factors.csv` | 12 factors | Relative risk, evidence levels, modifiability |
| `breast_cancer_survival_by_stage.csv` | 20 entries | Survival rates by stage × income region |

## 🔑 Key Statistics

- **2.3 million** women diagnosed worldwide in 2022
- **670,000** breast cancer deaths in 2022
- **>90%** five-year survival in high-income countries
- **40%** in South Africa, **66%** in India
- Projected **40% rise** in incidence by 2050
- #1 cause of cancer death in women in **110+ countries**

## 🎯 WHO GBCI 60-60-80 Targets

| Pillar | Target | Goal |
|--------|--------|------|
| 🏥 Early Detection | **60%** | Invasive cancers diagnosed at Stage I or II |
| ⏱️ Timely Diagnosis | **60 days** | Diagnosis within 60 days of presentation |
| 💊 Treatment | **80%** | Patients completing recommended treatment |
